Bengal Cat

The Bengal cat is a domesticated cat breed created from hybrids of domestic cats and the Asian leopard cat.

  • Scientific Name: Prionailurus bengalensis
  • Size: Average to large-sized.
  • Weight: Males 5-8 kg, females 4-5 kg.
  • Grooming: They produce lower than average levels of allergens
  • Temperament: Smart, energetic and playful.
